## Organization

- **Reporting Folders**: `Rapport hebdomadaire` and `Rapport mensuel` contain time-stamped performance reviews (e.g., Week 12 to 15, March 2026).
- **Templates**: The `templates/` folder ensures consistency across weekly, monthly, and quarterly reports.
- **Resources**: The `resources/` directory is highly categorized, containing subfolders for specific business domains such as vendor tracking (`suivi vendeur`), priority ratios (`ratios prioritaires`), and defect management (`defectuosite`).

The structure suggests a template-driven workflow where data from the `resources/` categories is synthesized into the periodic reports.
